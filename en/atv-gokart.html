What you will see on your ATV and Go Kart tour in Paracas

The ATV (quad bike) and Go Kart experience in Paracas is perfect for speed and adventure lovers. You will drive all-terrain vehicles through the golden dunes of the desert with total safety, feeling the thrill of dominating the sand while enjoying unique landscapes at the best price.

Our Go Kart circuit offers a professional karting experience with high-speed karts. The track is designed to provide maximum excitement, with challenging curves and straights where you can demonstrate your speed.

No prior experience is necessary. Before starting, you will receive complete training on how to handle the vehicles. Our team of certified guides and instructors will accompany you throughout the experience to ensure your safety and fun.
