ATV & Go Kart

Conquer the desert dunes at full speed.

Adrenaline in the Desert

The ATV (quad bike) and Go Kart experience in Paracas is perfect for speed and adventure lovers. You will drive all-terrain vehicles through the golden dunes of the desert, feeling the thrill of dominating the sand while enjoying unique landscapes.

Our Go Kart circuit offers a professional karting experience with high-speed karts. The track is designed to provide maximum excitement, with challenging curves and straights where you can demonstrate your speed.

No prior experience is necessary. Before starting, you will receive complete training on how to handle the vehicles. Our team of instructors will accompany you throughout the experience to ensure your safety and fun.
