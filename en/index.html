HELP CENTER

Resolve your doubts

1. How do I make my reservation?

You can start your reservation via WhatsApp. As they are personalized experiences, we first coordinate the details with you to ensure an adventure tailored to your needs. Once the plan is defined, the payment is made online securely.

2. What is the cancellation policy?

We understand unforeseen events. Reservations can be canceled or rescheduled with prior coordination. By confirming your online payment, you guarantee your spot for the agreed date.

3. Is it safe to travel with SolyMar?

Absolutely. Safety is our priority. We operate with expert guides and certified equipment, maintaining a record of zero risk in all our activities.

4. What should I bring to the tours?

We always recommend sunscreen, water, and comfortable clothes. For specific activities like diving or trekking, we will provide you with a detailed list at the time of your reservation.
