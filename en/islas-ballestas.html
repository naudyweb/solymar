Ballestas Islands

Discover the archipelago that is home to the richest marine biodiversity on the Peruvian coast.

The Jewel of the Pacific

The Ballestas Islands are known as the "Galapagos of Peru". This archipelago is home to an incredible diversity of marine life and guano birds.

During the tour in a modern boat, you will be able to observe sea lions, Humboldt penguins and thousands of birds in their protected natural habitat.

The tour includes a view of the mysterious "Candelabro", a giant geoglyph carved into the sand that can only be appreciated from the sea.
