What you will see on your Ballestas Islands tour

The Ballestas Islands are an archipelago located off the coast of Paracas, in the Ica region. Known as the "Galapagos of Peru," these islands are home to an incredible diversity of marine life and sea birds, offering a unique experience with total safety at the best price.

The tour to the Ballestas Islands is an unmissable experience during your visit to Paracas. During the boat trip, you will be able to observe sea lions, Humboldt penguins, pelicans, frigate birds, and a wide variety of sea birds with our certified guides. The rock formations of the islands create a unique and impressive landscape.

The islands are also famous for the "Candelabro", a huge geoglyph figure carved into the side of one of the islands, visible only from the sea. This archaeological mystery adds an element of history and adventure to your tour of the National Reserve.
