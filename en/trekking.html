Golden Shadows Trek

Walk through ancient cliffs and golden dunes at sunset.

A Walk Between Two Worlds

The Golden Shadows Trek is one of the most magical experiences in the Paracas National Reserve. This hike takes you through millennial desert landscapes, following trails that wind between huge cliffs and the Pacific Ocean.

The best time for this trekking is in the afternoon, when the sun begins to set and the light turns the dunes a vibrant golden color, creating the famous "golden shadows." It is a unique opportunity for nature and photography lovers.

Our expert guides will share stories about the geology and history of the region while leading you safely through the most impressive viewpoints of the reserve.
