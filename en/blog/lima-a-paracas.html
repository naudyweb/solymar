How to get to Paracas from Lima: 3 safe and comfortable ways

Paracas is the perfect escape from the capital. Just 260 km south of Lima, this paradise of biodiversity and adventure awaits you. If you are in tourist areas like Miraflores, Barranco, or Downtown Lima, here we explain the safest options for your trip.

Your transport guide for safe travel

Planning your transfer is the first step for an unforgettable experience. At SolyMar Paracas, we care about your safety from the moment you leave home until you reach our activity center operated by certified guides.

1. Premium Category Bus (Most Recommended)

It is the most popular and safest way for travelers. Companies like Cruz del Sur, Oltursa, or PeruBus (VIP Service) offer modern and safe terminals.

  • From Miraflores/Barranco: Most of these companies have stations near the Vía Expresa, just 15-20 minutes by taxi.
  • Advantages: 160° reclining seats, monitored GPS, relieved drivers, and strict luggage control.
  • Time: Approximately 3.5 to 4 hours.

2. Private Transport or Tourist Shuttles

If you are looking for maximum comfort and personalization, private transport is ideal, especially for groups or families.

  • Door to door: They pick you up directly at your hotel or Airbnb and drop you off at the door of SolyMar Paracas.
  • Safety: You avoid public terminals and travel only with your inner circle.
  • Flexibility: You can request stops at points of interest like Chincha to try the local gastronomy.

3. Own Car or Rental

For those who love the freedom of the road, driving down the Panamericana Sur is an excellent option. The highway is in very good condition.

  • Route: Simply follow the Panamericana Sur to km 245 (Pisco/Paracas interchange).
  • Recommendation: Avoid traveling at night if you don't know the route. Tolls accept cash and cards.
  • Parking: In Paracas, most hotels and safe areas have 24/7 surveillance.
