Diving in Paracas

Dive into the depths of the Pacific and discover a fascinating underwater world.

Explore the Depths

Diving in Paracas is a unique experience that allows you to explore the deep waters of the Pacific and discover the rich marine biodiversity of the Paracas National Reserve. With pleasant temperatures all year round and excellent visibility, Paracas is the perfect destination for divers of all levels.

During your dive, you will be able to observe sea lions, sea turtles, rays, and a wide variety of tropical fish. The rock formations and shipwrecks create a perfect ecosystem for marine life.

No prior experience is needed for the scuba diving baptism. Our certified instructors will teach you everything you need to enjoy yourself safely and fun.
