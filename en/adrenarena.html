Adrenarena Park

The ultimate adventure park in Paracas with ziplines, kayaks, and unlimited adrenaline.

Adventure Without Limits

Adrenarena is the most complete adventure park in the Ica region. It offers an adrenaline-filled experience for the whole family with activities that combine nature and excitement.

The park features more than 20 different activities, from high-speed ziplines to kayaking in natural lagoons. A perfect place for strong emotions.

Designed to spend the whole day exploring. We have rest areas and restaurants for your greatest comfort and fun.
