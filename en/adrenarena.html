Adrenarena Park

Maximum adrenaline in the most exciting park in the desert.

Limitless Adventure

Adrenarena is the most complete adventure park in the Ica region. Located in a privileged natural environment, it offers an adrenaline-filled experience for the whole family with activities that combine nature, excitement, and safety.

The park features more than 20 different activities, from high-speed ziplines to kayaking in natural lagoons. It is the perfect place for those seeking thrills without sacrificing safety - all equipment is state-of-the-art and there are instructors at every station.

The concept of Adrenarena is designed so you can spend the whole day exploring all the activities. There are rest areas, restaurants, and picnic areas to make the experience comfortable and fun.
