Paragliding in Paracas

Fly over the desert and the ocean in an unforgettable aerial adventure with certified guides.

Feel the Freedom of Flight

Paragliding in Paracas is a unique experience that allows you to see the desert and ocean from the heights. We take off from Playa Supay or Cerro El Maldito.

Our tandem flight is perfect for beginners. You will fly with a certified instructor enjoying between 5 to 12 minutes of pure adrenaline.

During the flight you will observe the golden dunes, the coastline and the majesty of the National Reserve from a privileged perspective.
