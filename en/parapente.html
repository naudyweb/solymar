Paragliding in Paracas

Feel the freedom of flying over the desert and the ocean.

Feel the Freedom of Flight

Paragliding in Paracas is an extraordinary adventure that offers a bird's-eye view of where the golden desert meets the deep blue ocean. Our tandem flights take off from two iconic locations within the Paracas National Reserve: Playa Supay and Cerro El Maldito hill.

Depending on the prevailing wind conditions, our expert pilots select the optimal takeoff point to ensure a safe and breathtaking experience. Both spots offer unparalleled panoramic views of the entire reserve, allowing you to contemplate the landscape from a truly unique perspective.

Our tandem flight is perfect for beginners. No previous experience is needed - you will fly alongside a certified instructor who will handle the navigation while you simply enjoy the freedom of flight.
