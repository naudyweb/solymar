Paragliding in Paracas

Feel the freedom of flying over the desert and the ocean.

Feel the Freedom of Flight

Paragliding in Paracas is an extraordinary adventure that offers a bird's-eye view of where the golden desert meets the deep blue ocean. Depending on the wind conditions to ensure the best panoramic views of the Paracas National Reserve, we select between two strategic takeoff points: Playa Supay and the Cerro El Maldito hill.

Our tandem flight is perfect for beginners. No previous experience is needed - you will fly alongside a certified instructor who will handle the navigation while you enjoy 5 to 12 minutes of pure adrenaline. Takeoff and landing at Playa Supay or Cerro El Maldito are smooth and safe, ensuring a high-quality professional experience.

During the flight, you can observe the golden dunes of the desert, the Paracas coastline, and the majesty of the National Reserve. It is a privileged perspective that only free flight can offer.
