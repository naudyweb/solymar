Paragliding in Paracas

Feel the freedom of flying over the desert and the ocean.

Feel the Freedom of Flight

Paragliding in Paracas is a unique experience that allows you to contemplate the desert and the ocean from the heights. With ideal weather conditions all year round, Paracas is one of the best destinations in Peru for this sport.

Our tandem flight is perfect for beginners. No previous experience is needed - you will fly alongside a certified instructor who will control the sail while you enjoy the experience.
