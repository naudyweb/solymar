National Reserve

Explore the majestic contrast where the driest desert meets the blue Pacific Ocean.

A Natural Sanctuary

The Paracas National Reserve is the largest natural sanctuary on the Peruvian coast. On this tour, we will explore breathtaking landscapes where the desert and the ocean unite in a unique harmony.

Accompanied by a certified guide, you will discover the fascinating geology of the area and the rich biodiversity that inhabits this protected ecosystem.
