Paracas National Reserve

The majestic contrast where the world's driest desert meets the ocean.

The Largest Natural Sanctuary

The Paracas National Reserve is the largest natural sanctuary on the Peruvian coast. On this land tour, we will explore breathtaking landscapes where the desert and the ocean unite in a unique harmony.

Detailed Itinerary

Our tour begins at 10:30 AM and lasts approximately 3 hours:

  • info
    Interpretation Center

    Visit the RNP information center to understand the local ecosystem.

  • landscape
    The Cathedral (La Catedral)

    Visit this famous natural rock formation, an icon of Paracas.

  • beach_access
    Iconic Beaches

    Panoramic tour of Red Beach (Playa Roja) and Yumaque Beach.

  • visibility
    Hike & Observation

    Visit the Flamingo viewpoint and observe marine fossils.

  • restaurant
    Lagunillas Beach

    Time for lunch and enjoying the ocean (optional).
