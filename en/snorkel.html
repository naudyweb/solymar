Snorkeling in Paracas

Explore the crystal-clear waters of the Paracas National Reserve and discover the marine life of the Pacific.

An Unforgettable Experience

Snorkeling in Paracas is one of the most popular activities to enjoy Peru's marine nature. The Paracas National Reserve is home to extraordinary marine biodiversity, with crystal-clear waters that allow you to observe sea lions, penguins, pelicans, and a wide variety of tropical fish up close.

During this experience, our certified guides will take you to the best snorkeling spots, where you can swim alongside marine fauna in their natural habitat. The mild Pacific waters offer ideal conditions for this activity all year round.

No prior experience is required for this activity. Our team will provide you with all the necessary snorkeling equipment and give you a brief instruction before entering the water.
