Snorkeling in Paracas

Explore the crystal-clear waters of the National Reserve and discover marine life with total safety.

An Unforgettable Experience

Snorkeling in Paracas is one of the best ways to experience Peru's marine nature. The National Reserve is home to extraordinary biodiversity.

Our certified guides will take you to the best spots, where you can swim alongside sea lions and penguins in their natural habitat.

No prior experience is required. Our team will provide all the necessary equipment and training.
